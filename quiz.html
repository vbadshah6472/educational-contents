Competition Rules

  • There are 4 teams: Red, Blue, Green, and Yellow
  • General Round: 40 questions worth 5 points each
  • Audio-Visual Round: 12 questions worth 10 points each
  • Rapid-Fire Round: 5 questions per team in 40 seconds
  • Timer decreases when questions are passed (40s → 30s → 20s → 10s)
  • Only the active team can answer during their turn
  • Use PASS button to move to next team if current team can't answer
