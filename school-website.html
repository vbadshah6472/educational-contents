About Our School

Welcome to Shree Tikuligadh Secondary School

Established in 2018, Shree Tikuligadh Secondary School has been providing quality education to the children of Tilottama-14, Rupandehi and surrounding areas. Our mission is to create a learning environment that fosters academic excellence, character development, and social responsibility.

We offer classes from grade 1 to 10, following the curriculum prescribed by the Government of Nepal. Our dedicated faculty and modern facilities ensure that every student receives the best possible education.

Our Academic Programs

We offer comprehensive academic programs designed to prepare students for success in higher education and beyond.

Primary Level (1-5)

Foundational education focusing on literacy, numeracy, and holistic development.

  • Nepali, English, Mathematics
  • Science, Social Studies
  • Computer Basics

Lower Secondary (6-8)

Building on foundational knowledge with more specialized subjects.

  • All core subjects
  • Optional Mathematics
  • Computer Science

Secondary Level (9-10)

Preparation for SEE with comprehensive subject options.

  • Science, Management, Humanities
  • Computer Science
  • SEE Preparation Classes

Admission Process

Join our community of learners for the academic year 2081 BS.

Eligibility

  • Grade 1: Minimum age 5 years
  • Other grades: Transfer certificate from previous school
  • Original birth certificate

Documents Required

  • Birth certificate (original + 2 copies)
  • Recent passport photos (4 copies)
  • Previous school report card (for grades 2-10)

Fee Structure

  • Admission fee: Rs. 2,000 (one-time)
  • Monthly tuition: Rs. 1,500 - Rs. 2,500 (varies by grade)
  • Transportation fee: Rs. 1,000/month (optional)
